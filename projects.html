OmniMirror – AI-Powered Smart Mirror

OmniMirror is a smart mirror that integrates AI, facial recognition, voice commands, and real-time data to provide personalized experiences for users. It helps you stay updated on weather, news, reminders, and more – all while seamlessly blending into your home.

Technologies Used

  • **Hardware**: Raspberry Pi, Camera, Microphone, Two-Way Mirror Display
  • **Software**: AWS Rekognition, OpenCV, Google Speech-to-Text, MagicMirror²
  • **Languages**: Python, JavaScript, SQLite

Key Features

  • ✔ **Personalized Display**: Based on facial recognition
  • ✔ **Voice Interaction**: Custom wake word and voice control
  • ✔ **Real-Time Content**: Weather, calendar events, news, and more
  • ✔ **AI Recommendations**: Learns your preferences over time

Future Plans

In the future, I plan to integrate even more advanced AI features like emotion recognition, enhance the user interface, and integrate with smart home devices for a fully connected experience.
